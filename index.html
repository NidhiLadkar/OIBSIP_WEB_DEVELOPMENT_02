Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was one of the most loved Presidents of India and a visionary scientist. He was born on 15 October 1931 and dedicated his life to education, innovation, and national development.

Known as the "Missile Man of India", he played a pivotal role in India's space and missile programs. He was also a great teacher, author, and motivator who inspired millions of students with his speeches and books.

His humility, intelligence, and commitment to serving the country made him an icon of leadership. His famous book "Wings of Fire" continues to inspire youth across the nation.

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action." – Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam
